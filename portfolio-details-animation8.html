Short Bugatti Animation

Home Work

For this project I downloaded a free model of a Bugatti Chiron 2017 made available by kimzauto on the Free3D website, furthermore I also imported a rough model of a desert terrain from the Free3D platform made available by aaron_g_randall, finally I imported the HDRI from HDRI Heaven made available by Greg Zaal.
I rigged the car's wheels with a driver so when the car will be moved with a null object the wheels will turn automatically
Furthermore I added a steering rig, thus meaning that when the cars will be rotated along the Z axis the wheels will turn along the same axis.
I also added a floor constraint thus meaning that the surface which the car sits on has slight variations the wheels of the car will still adapt to it. I also added a path which the car follows automatically, therefore I animated the tilt of the car body to fit the momentum of the vehicule.
This is mainly a rigging and animating project since no modelling or shading has been done.
I created multiple cameras to record the action and switch them inside Blender. I exported the project to a Render Farm called Rebus Farm and set the render engine to Cycle.
I imported the footage into Premiere and added SFX.

  • Tools: Blender and Premiere
