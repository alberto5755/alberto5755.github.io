Interview of the Journal member

This project is a documentary based on interviews of member of the Gewilo's Journal, in cantonese Gweilo means outsider. In fact, this online journal has been created by students of the French International School of Hong Kong.
All the shots of the inteview were recorded in Causewaybay, since we decided it is one of the most representative neighbourhoods of Hong Kong .
The color correcton, sound design and overall editing has been done in Premiere, for this project I also included footage that I captured previously such as Timelapses and Drone footage.
