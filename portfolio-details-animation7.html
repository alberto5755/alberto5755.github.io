Hovering PBR Spher

Home Work

This project is a simple animaton of a hovering sphere.
I applied a image texture node and enabled Displacement and Bump, therefore the texture would affect the geometry of the sphere.
I then keyframed the camera and parented to a null object in order to make a perfect rotation around the sphere.
I exported the image texture for free for the texture heavens website.
I imported a plane and turn the roughness to zero in orded to have a reflective plane for the animation.
I added two spotlights with complementary colors and rendered in Cycles.

  • Tools: Blender
  • Completed: 2 Jun 2020
