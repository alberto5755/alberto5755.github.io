Green Screen Fight shot

This short video is a keying and compositing exercise where I had to create a short fight scene on After Effect.
I filmed myself two times walking in the opposite direction, dodging and doing an uppercut and keyed myself out. The background that was included was an image from creative commons, as well as the smoke, the rain, the blood spills, stains and sound fx.

  • Tools: After Effect and Premiere
  • Completed: 24 May 2019
