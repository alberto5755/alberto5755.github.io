Colosseum AR App

This project has been created to showcase a multisensorial experience.
In fact I wanted to experience with Augmented Reality, and therefore creating an app where the user could visualize the Colosseum that is projected to the environment.
I downloaded the creative common model of the colosseum and edited it on blender, I then imported it on Unity. I then chose an image target wheere the colosseum could be attached to. I export it for an iOS device, as a XCode file, I then plugged my iPhone and hit Run after setting my Apple ID as a developper.
The project can be downloaded here

  • Tools: Blender, Unity, Vuforia and XCode
  • Completed: 12 Feb 2019
