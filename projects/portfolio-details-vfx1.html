Cybernetic Hand
Composited

Home Work

I utilized the Cybernetic hand model on 5 different static shots, in which all of them required object matchmoving, masking, chroma keying, roto, cleanup and compositing.

The process is very similar for all the 5 shots, I did a planar tracking of the actor's hand, exported the keyframes into Blender, parent the cybernetic hand model to the tracking points, adjust the rotation to match the footage and animate the fingers.

I then added a light to match the lighting of the shot and rendered.

On After Effects, I keyed out the real hand, and painted the background layer to create a clean plate.

I finally composited the CG plate and the clean plate with RGB curves, color correction, feathered black solids for the drop shadows and motion blur. The SFX were later added in the Editing process and are available in the movie.

I also made other VFX shots where I had to replace the arm of the actor with a CG stomp arm, and another where a CG Atm gets punched, however I didn't modelled these assets.

These shots were then used in an interactive short movie named Almost Human based from this poster which can be viewed at the bottom of this page.
