Tracked Stabilisation
sequence

Home Work

This short sequence is a compilation of various locked stabilisations. The process would reside in taking a picture every half-meter of the subject. Then import every picture as an image sequence and perform a motion tracking with a high contrast point on After Effects.
Once the data has been collected, I have saved it withing a null object and parent it to a virtual camera. The footage would be then calibrated to the high contrast point that I chose.
Some shots are from Florences statues and other various buildings.
I imported all the footage and added sound effects on Premiere

  • Tools: After Effect and Premiere
  • Completed: 24 May 2020
