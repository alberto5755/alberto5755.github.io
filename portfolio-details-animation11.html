Exploding Rock

Home Work

For this project is used a icosphere with a subvision modifier, I then used a cell fracture quick effect to breake up the sphere into different pieces.
I applied a rock texture from texture heaven to the sphere and created a force field in the center of the sphere, keyframed its intensity and turned on rigid body physics.
Furthermore I added a point light with a orange hue at the same position as the force field.
I added a cube with volumetric shading to create a fog and glow coming from the light. Finally I added sound effects on Premiere.

  • Tools: Blender and Premiere
  • Completed: 19 Mars 2020
